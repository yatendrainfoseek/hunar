About Hunar

  1. Home
  2. About Hunar

Hunar, the first multi designer house of the city of Nawabs- Lucknow, spread across 6000sqft of space in the heart of city pursues a new design concept and promises a premium customer-oriented experience with a high level of personalization and exclusivity. Housing over 30 designers with a strong focus on emerging and advanced designers, the store will go above and beyond to reflect the brand’s commitment to giving a unique experience to its shoppers.

Unlike any other store in Lucknow, Hunar will focus on rotating in store merchandise as fast as every 2 weeks. We are a trend driven fashion house of young stylists and designers providing a one stop solution to all shopping and styling needs.

Shoppers can sign up for private styling consultations and indulge in a luxurious treatment or can instantly pick a style from the prêt collection readily available.

Our Designer Collection Company Proffile

Our goal is to transform your wardrobe tailored to your personality and preferences. One of the many things that would set this unique destination apart is the 7 by 10 feet wall with a video display of new collections. The design is welcoming, curation unique and it would be quite simply a great place to shop for apparel, jewellery, accessories, kids and home & living as well.

Hunar is an initiative to explore fashion and style through a series of themes and nurture talent and make designer wear accessible to the people of Lucknow. We will be providing designers with exposure and growth opportunities and our guests a chance to explore and discover the new.
